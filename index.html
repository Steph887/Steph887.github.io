Introduction to otters

Think of an animal that spends the majority of their time in the water, extremely social, and has an insatiable curiosity. Meet the otter! Known to be extremely playful animals, amazing swim skills, and who are big time fish lovers! Otters have thick, brown, waterproof fur and webbed feet. They are extremely vital to their ecosystems but have also become famous for holding hands while they sleep, a habit that helps them stay closely together within their family groups.
